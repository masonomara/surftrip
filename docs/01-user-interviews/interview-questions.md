# Docket User Interviews

## Target Personas

**1. Small/Mid Firm Managing Partner:**

Partner or owner at a 5-25 attorney firm who makes purchasing decisions, understands compliance burden,lacks enterprise IT. Good for evaluating pricing and need, as well as trust profile with AI.

**2. Legal Clinic Director:**

Lead at a law school clinic or legal aid. High volume, low resources, trains new lawyers constantly. Good for training and onboarding pain points, how "best practices" actually get documented, permissions with rotating staff/students, where they cut corners.

**3. Senior Paralegal / Legal Assistant:**

Daily Clio user, law firm or clinic experience. Understands Clio workarounds and frustration, every time-wasting click. Good fo understandign what actually happens vs what partenrs think, real daily workflows and pain points, how Teams/Slack is actually used for case discussions, and trainig new hires.

**4. Legal Malpractice / Ethics Attorney:**

Practices legal malpractice defense or serves on state bar ethics committee. Knows what goes wrong when case management fails. Understands how privilege, conflicts, retention requirements across jurisdictions shoudl work. Can validate compliance gaps.
