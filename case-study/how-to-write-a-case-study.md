## Product Lifecycle Items

The following are things to explore with calrifying questions if you are struggliing to find thisngs to write about from the proejct documentation or codebase:

1. Problem/Context

   - What problems existed, who had it
   - Business goal or user need driving the project
   - Constraints (budget, timeline, technical)

2. Research and Discover

   - How I understood the problem (user interviews, compitor analysis, stakeholder conversations)
   - Key insights that shaped direction

3. Design process

   - Information architecture, wireframes, key design decisions
   - Why I made spefici choices (tradeoffs, rationale)
   - Iterations based on feedback

4. Technical arhitecture (keep brief)

   - Architecture decisons and why
   - Technologies chosen and reasoning
   - Interestign challenges to avoid

5. Outcomes

   - Metrics
   - User feedback, quotes
   - What shipped and what impact it had

6. Reflection
   - What you learned, what youd do differently
