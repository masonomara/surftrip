**Get Your Finances Right From the Start: A Checklist for Launching Your Law Firm**

#### Starting a new law firm involves tackling a comprehensive to-do list. You’ll need to build a solid business plan, get your finances in order, and acquire the right tools to achieve your goals. Set yourself up for success by establishing these foundations from the outset, as it’s far simpler than trying to course correct later on.

###### This checklist will guide you through the financial aspects of launching your firm—covering everything from billing and accounting to the powerful role that legal technologies play in keeping you efficient and organized every step of the way.


**Get Your Finances Right From the Start: A Checklist for Launching Your Law Firm**

##### **STEP ONE**
## **Decide what you want to** **charge for your services**

Determining your hourly rate will help position your new law firm
within its respective market. Your rate influences how clients perceive
the value of your services and whether they ultimately choose to hire
you, so setting the right price for your services is crucial. Here’s what
to consider:

Determine how you want to charge clients for your services
(e.g., hourly, flat fee, contingency).

Set a competitive rate that reflects the value you provide
without underselling yourself.

Avoid overpricing, which could deter potential clients
and hinder growth.

Use industry benchmarks—such as resources like
[“How Much Should I Charge As a Lawyer?”—for guidance.](https://www.clio.com/resources/legal-trends/compare-lawyer-rates/)

##### **STEP TWO**
## **Establish a seamless** **billing process**

Efficient billing practices are essential to avoid cash flow bottlenecks
and ensure timely payments from clients. After all, the faster you get
your bills out, the faster (and more likely) you are to get paid.

Fortunately, several tools are available to make the billing process
effortless. Use an online legal payment solution like Clio Manage to:

Create branded invoices with your logo for enhanced recognition.

Generate multiple bills in one click and easily edit and apply
discounts as needed.

Send automated reminders to prevent payment delays.

Providing convenient payment channels, including website

##### **STEP FOUR**
## **Master accounting** **processes**

Accounting is fundamental to running any business—and law firms
are no exception. Firms need to have a solid handle on their accounting
to not only ensure that they are running their business efficiently but
also to ensure they are meeting their trust compliance obligations.

Sound accounting practices provide financial transparency, ensure
compliance with jurisdictional requirements, and help you make
informed business decisions. Simplify legal accounting processes

Using just one solution to accept trust deposits, complete trust
