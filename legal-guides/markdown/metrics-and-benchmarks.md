## **Steps for Calculating Lockup in Clio**

**01.** **[In Clio Manage, go to Reports](https://app.clio.com/nc/#/reports?tab=1)**


**02.** **[Select Accounts Receivable Report](https://app.clio.com/nc/#/reports/accounts_receivable)**


     - Set parameters to all clients, all responsible attorneys, all practice areas

     - Set the time frame to your last billing period, i.e. last month

     - Run the report

     - Record the **Accounts Receivable Total**


**03.** **[Select Work in Progress Report](https://app.clio.com/nc/#/reports/work_in_progress)**

     - Set parameters to all clients, all responsible attorneys, all practice areas

     - Set the time frame to your last billing period, i.e. last month

     - Run the report

     - Record the **Work in Progress Total**


**04.** **[Select the Billing History](https://app.clio.com/nc/#/reports/billing_history)**


     - Set parameters to all clients, all responsible attorneys, all practice areas

     - Set the time frame to Last Year (Jan to Dec)

     - Run the report

     - Under Grand Totals, record the **Amount**


**05.** **Use the formulas for Realization Lockup,**

**Collection Lockup, and Total Lockup in the table**

To improve performance, your firm needs a clear understanding of its key
metrics. Use the firm metrics table provided in this resource to calculate the
KPIs listed below and establish your benchmark values. These metrics allow
you to track progress and make informed decisions about where to optimize

## **Financial Performance**

**REALIZATION RATE**


_Include in quarterly billing performance reviews with all_
_relevant stakeholders._

Spot inefficiencies in billing practices and reduce unnecessary
[write-downs. Firms using Clio’s outstanding balances reminders—](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9285345945243-Bill-Reminders#h_01JVDCTDTV0T07TZG0TTMSF8EB)
[to follow up with unpaid bills—and bulk billing](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9285169278747-Generate-Bills-for-Billing-in-Clio#h_01HRFD1MM64ZC0RJCS3CRJZ7QF) had realization rates
that were 26% and 21% higher, respectively, than firms that did not
[use these features. Refer to industry benchmarks to see how your](https://www.clio.com/resources/legal-trends/benchmarks/)
firm stacks up against others your size.


**COLLECTION RATE**


_Make this part of your regular monthly financial review._

Examine aged receivables and client-specific trends to improve
follow-up and payment consistency. Firms using Clio’s [email bill](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9285343645595-Send-Bills-for-Billing-in-Clio)
[sharing had 3% higher collection rates, and among all firms that](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9285343645595-Send-Bills-for-Billing-in-Clio)

[per lawyer each month vs. firms that did not. Refer to industry](https://www.clio.com/resources/legal-trends/benchmarks/)
[benchmarks to see how your firm stacks up against others your size.](https://www.clio.com/resources/legal-trends/benchmarks/)

## **Operational Efficiency**

**UTILIZATION RATE**


_Review weekly with relevant stakeholders._

Set minimum targets and assess time management to improve
billable performance. Low utilization might suggest distractions,
[poor task management, or unclear priorities. Use Clio Duo](https://www.clio.com/web/customer-duo/) to assist
with document review and task prioritization, freeing up more time
[to focus on client work. Refer to industry benchmarks to see how](https://www.clio.com/resources/legal-trends/benchmarks/)
your firm stacks up against others your size.


**LOCKUP DAYS**


_Review monthly for financial operations planning._

Use baseline to experiment with quicker billing workflows in
Clio Manage (e.g. using [automated bill reminders,](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9285345945243-Bill-Reminders#h_01JVDCTDTV0T07TZG0TTMSF8EB) [bill themes,](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9285055886235-Bill-Themes-and-Customizations)
[bill message templates) and encourage online payment to improve](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9285343645595-Send-Bills-for-Billing-in-Clio#h_01HBC5VHWE98P0DJXMT5PSK4YN)
cash flow. Law firms accepting online payments reduce total
lockup by four days.


**TASK COMPLETION RATE**


_Review in weekly team standups or assistant check-ins._

Flag overdue work with individuals and highlight task management
in performance reviews to encourage operational discipline.


**INTAKE CONVERSION RATE**


_Review monthly with relevant stakeholders._

Diagnose drop-off points in your intake process to unlock
bottlenecks and apply consistent processes to improve conversions.
Consider automating time-intensive tasks like scheduling,
reminders, and intake with a tool like [Clio Grow—firms that do have](https://www.clio.com/grow/)
seen up to 51% more leads and 52% more revenue.



**CONSULTATION NO-SHOW RATE**


_Review weekly and evaluate emerging trends each month._

Analyzing no-show data can help identify trends, implement
preventative measures, and optimize appointment scheduling.
A high no-show rate can indicate issues with scheduling,
communication, or client fit—potentially impacting revenue
and workload. Consider a scheduling tool (e.g. [Clio Scheduler)](https://www.clio.com/features/appointment-bookings/)
to automate bookings.


**TIME TO INTAKE COMPLETION**


_Review monthly in admin performance planning._

Set a 1–2 day benchmark, identify where the process slows
(e.g., scheduling, missing form completion deadlines,
back-and-forth communication) and optimize based on
[bottlenecks. Using Clio Grow](https://www.clio.com/grow/) to automate intake helps
firms onboard clients an average of four days faster.


**MOST PROFITABLE MATTER TYPES**


_Review quarterly in finance planning._

Align marketing and intake strategy toward your most
profitable case categories.


**MATTER TYPE DISTRIBUTION**


_Review bi-annually in strategy planning._

Use this insight to assess if your firm’s matter mix aligns
with your strategic goals and revenue targets.

## **Team Productivity**

**CASEWORK VOLUME**


_Review weekly with management._

Redistribute caseloads to balance effort and maintain
service standards.


**BILLABLE VS. NON-BILLABLE HOURS**


_Review monthly in 1:1s and performance reviews._

Identify and offload low-value tasks for team members that
can be delegated or automated.


**REVENUE PER LAWYER**


_Review monthly in 1:1 meetings with lawyers._

Use this metric for goal setting, evaluating productivity
performance, bonus structure discussions, and identifying
outliers in productivity who require coaching.


**AVERAGE CASE DURATION**


_Discuss quarterly in legal team reviews._

Set case benchmarks to learn from top performers and fix
workflow issues with underperformers whose cases fall behind.


**ONLINE PAYMENT ADOPTION RATE**


_Review monthly in finance planning._

Encourage more online payments by explaining benefits to
clients and embedding payment links in invoices. Firms using
[Clio Payments are paid 15 days faster, and are 2.5x more likely](https://app.clio.com/nc/#/online_payments)
to be paid within 7 days, vs. firms that have manual billing only.



**AVERAGE RESPONSE TIME**


_Review monthly in operations meetings._

Establish internal response time SLAs and use them as a service
excellence metric in performance evaluations. If team members
consistently fall below targets, provide coaching and retraining.
Tools like text messaging and online client portals can help you
respond faster and keep clients engaged. On average, 87% of firms
using [Clio’s text messaging respond within an hour, and 56% of](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9125220988827-Text-Messaging-in-Clio-Manage)
those messages get a client reply within five minutes. Messages
[sent through Clio for Clients](https://help.clio.com/hc/en-us/articles/9043484357147-Clio-for-Clients-Law-Firm-Setup) see similar results—nearly 90% get
a response within an hour, with over half answered in just
five minutes.
