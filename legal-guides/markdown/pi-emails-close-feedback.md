## Email Templates for Personal Injury Firms:
# **Closing Cases and** **Seeking Feedback**


Email Templates for Personal Injury Firms: Closing Cases and Seeking Feedback

##### Clear, professional communication when closing your case ensures your client understands the final steps, receives necessary documents, and has an opportunity to share their feedback.

###### These email templates help you wrap up a case smoothly— whether confirming a final settlement, requesting a release of claims, or inviting clients to provide feedback. They provide a structured yet approachable way to ensure your clients feel informed, supported, and appreciated as their case comes to a close.



_Tip:_



To make the best use of the templates in this guide, make sure to review
them and tailor them based on the services your firm provides. Based on the
situation, you may also want to combine messaging from multiple templates
into a separate communication.


Remember that these templates are provided for educational purposes only.
Lawyers should always use professional judgment when using or revising these
templates, which may require amendments based on your jurisdiction and the
needs of your particular file.

###### **EMAIL TEMPLATE #1**
### **Final settlement agreement**

When a personal injury case reaches a final settlement, it’s important to inform your
client of the outcome and provide them with the necessary paperwork to finalize the
process. This email notifies the client that the settlement has been concluded and outlines
the next steps.

You will want to emphasize the importance of reviewing and signing the release of claims
form, reassure the client that their funds will be disbursed promptly, and offer assistance
if any questions arise.

Use this template to ensure that the client understands the settlement details and the
actions required to complete the process.


Subject: Final settlement agreement


Dear [CLIENT NAME],


We are pleased to inform you that the settlement for your personal injury case has been
finalized. As part of this agreement, we will be issuing a settlement check in the amount
of [AMOUNT].


This amount is based on:


           - Settlement amount: [AMOUNT]


           - [PERCENTAGE] contingency fee: [AMOUNT]


           - [LIST OF OFFICE EXPENSE ITEMS]: [EXPENSE AMOUNTS]


Please read through the terms of the settlement and confirm that you agree to the resolution
by signing the attached release of claims form. Once we receive your signed release, we will
proceed with the disbursement of funds.


We are here to answer any questions or concerns you may have. Once again, thank you for
trusting us to represent you in this matter.


Best regards


[YOUR NAME] | [YOUR POSITION]


[LAW FIRM NAME]

###### **EMAIL TEMPLATE #2**
### **Closing the case**

When closing your personal injury case, it is important to not only inform the client
of the case’s resolution but also gather feedback to ensure continued excellence in service.
This email is a closure notice and invites the client to participate in a client satisfaction
survey to assess the quality of legal representation provided.

With this notice, you will want to emphasize the firm’s appreciation for the client’s trust
and encourage honest feedback to improve future services. Use this template to express
genuine gratitude for the client’s time and insights and provide them access to the survey.


Subject: Closure of your personal injury case and client satisfaction survey


Dear [CLIENT NAME],


We are pleased to inform you that your personal injury case has been successfully
resolved. We would like to take this opportunity to express our gratitude for placing your
trust in [LAW FIRM NAME] during this challenging time.


We understand that the process may have been difficult and demanding, but we are honored
to have represented you and achieved a favorable outcome. Your satisfaction is of utmost
importance to us, and we hope that our services have met or exceeded your expectations.


To help us improve and continue delivering exceptional legal representation, we invite you to
take a few moments to complete a brief client satisfaction survey. Your honest feedback will
provide valuable insights into your experience with our firm and help us identify areas for
further improvement. Please find the survey attached to this email or access it through the
following link: [SURVEY LINK].


We genuinely appreciate your time and participation in this survey. Your feedback will
remain confidential and will be used solely for the purpose of enhancing our client services.
We value your opinion and assure you that your responses will make a difference.


Once again, it has been a privilege representing you in your personal injury case. We will
always be here for you should you require our legal expertise in the future. Thank you for
your trust and cooperation.


[YOUR NAME] | [YOUR POSITION]


[LAW FIRM NAME]


[PHONE NUMBER] | [EMAIL ADDRESS

###### **EMAIL TEMPLATE #3**
### **Client satisfaction survey follow-up**

Clients won’t always submit feedback when their case closes. Depending on the gravity
of their case, they might have too much to process at the time, or they may have just
forgotten. If you don’t receive any feedback from your client, it doesn’t hurt to send
out a gentle reminder that encourages them to share their thoughts and insights.


Subject: We value your feedback


Dear [CLIENT NAME],


Now that your personal injury case has been concluded, we want to thank you once again for
choosing [LAW FIRM NAME] to represent you. Your feedback is incredibly important to us as
we continue to improve our services.


Please take a moment to complete our brief client satisfaction survey, which should only take
a few minutes to finish. Your responses will help us better serve clients like you in the future.


[INSERT LINK TO SURVEY]


Thank you for your time and trust in our firm.

###### **EMAIL TEMPLATE #4**
### **Requesting a client review**

Positive client experiences are invaluable to your firm—not only do they reaffirm the
quality of your work, but they also help future clients feel confident in choosing you for
their legal needs. When a client expresses satisfaction with your services, it’s the perfect
opportunity to kindly ask them to leave a review on a site like Google, where it will be
seen by potential future clients. This email makes it easy for them to share their feedback
on Google, reinforcing your firm’s reputation and helping you continue to grow.


Subject: Share your experience with [LAW FIRM NAME]


Dear [CLIENT NAME],


We truly appreciate the trust you placed in [LAW FIRM NAME] to handle your case, and we’re
so glad to hear you had a positive experience working with us. Client feedback is incredibly
important to our firm—it helps others who may be facing similar legal challenges feel
confident in choosing the right representation.


If you have a moment, we would be grateful if you could share your experience by leaving a
review on Google. Your words can make a real difference for those in need of legal support.


[INSERT LINK TO GOOGLE REVIEW]


Thank you again for allowing us to support you through this process. If you ever need legal
assistance in the future, we are always here to help.


Best regards,
