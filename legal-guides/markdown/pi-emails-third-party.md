Email Templates for Personal Injury Firms: Third-Party Requests

##### With your personal injury case, collaborating with third parties can help uncover key digital evidence and provide valuable insights. Having readily available templates can enhance your emails’ efficiency while ensuring consistent, positive communication. These templates save time and help you deliver clear, reassuring, and helpful messages that demonstrate your commitment to handling your clients’ cases with the utmost care and attention.

###### These email correspondences cover a range of interactions on behalf of your client—from requesting authorizations and statements to providing case updates. Each message is an opportunity to guide your recipient through the process, keep them informed, and demonstrate your dedication to achieving the best possible outcome.



_Tip:_



To make the best use of the templates in this guide, make sure to review
them and tailor them based on the services your firm provides. Based on the
situation, you may also want to combine messaging from multiple templates
into a separate communication.


Remember that these templates are provided for educational purposes only.
Lawyers should always use professional judgment when using or revising these
templates, which may require amendments based on your jurisdiction and the
needs of your particular file.

Email Templates for Personal Injury Firms: Third-Party Requests

###### **EMAIL TEMPLATE #1**
### **Request for records/documentation**

When handling personal injury investigations, you’ll need to acquire your client’s
records and documentation from third-party organizations to ensure you fully
understand their situation. Once you get your client’s authorization, you can use
these templates to quickly reach out to the medical professionals, insurers,
employers, or whomever else you need to request information.

With this communication, you will want to emphasize the importance of obtaining
complete and timely documentation on behalf of your client, which will help you in
gathering all of your necessary evidence as efficiently as possible.

###### **EMAIL TEMPLATE #3**
### **Settlement offer and demand letter**

This template is designed for personal injury law firms to send a demand letter to the
insurance company of the defendant. This letter outlines the total amount of damages
and losses incurred by the plaintiff and requests compensation from the insurance company.
Additionally, the letter proposes a settlement offer as an attempt to avoid going to court.

Using this demand letter template helps your firm streamline the process of reaching
a fair settlement for their clients. It provides a structured format for presenting the
necessary information while adding value by demonstrating professionalism and
showcasing the firm’s commitment to achieving justice for its clients.

### **Request for witness statement**

This email template is intended for personal injury law firms to request witness statements
in relation to a personal injury case. It provides a clear and professional format for reaching
out to potential witnesses and requesting their cooperation in providing a statement
regarding the incident.

This template simplifies the process of requesting witness statements, ensuring
consistent communication, and facilitating the collection of key evidence necessary
for building a strong legal argument.

Email Templates for Personal Injury Firms: Third-Party Requests


Subject: Request for witness statement


Dear [WITNESS’S NAME],


I hope this email finds you well. I am reaching out to you on behalf of [YOUR LAW FIRM]
regarding a personal injury case that we are currently working on. We have been informed
that you were present at [LOCATION] on [DATE] and may have witnessed the incident that
resulted in our client’s injury.


As part of our efforts to build a strong legal argument and ensure a fair settlement for our
client, we kindly request your cooperation in providing a witness statement regarding the
incident. Your testimony and perspective as an eyewitness are crucial in establishing the facts
surrounding the occurrence and determining liability.


To assist you in providing the statement, we have attached a witness statement form to this
email. Please fill out the form to the best of your knowledge and recollection, providing as
much detail as possible about what you saw and heard. If you have any supporting documents
or photographs related to the incident, we would appreciate your inclusion of those as well.


We understand that this request may require some time and effort on your part, and we
genuinely appreciate your willingness to assist us in this matter. Your statement will greatly
contribute to our client’s case and help us seek a fair resolution for them.


Rest assured that all information provided will be treated with strict confidentiality and used
solely for the purpose of the legal proceedings related to this personal injury case.


Should you have any questions or concerns, or if there is anything we can do to facilitate this
process, please do not hesitate to contact me at [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS] or [YOUR PHONE
NUMBER]. We are here to assist you and are grateful for your cooperation in this matter.


Thank you for your time and consideration.


Best regards,
